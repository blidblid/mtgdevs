Wait, what are these numbers?
Power is average pick order.
If you only picked Black Lotus in vintage cube (lucky!), you'd have a power of 1.
If you only picked Demolish in WAR-draft, you'd have a power of 264, because there are 264 cards in WAR.
Mana is standard deviation of your mana symbols.
If it's high, your mana is all over the place.
If it's zero, you're playing mono-colored.
Curve is offset from a perfect curve.
What is a perfect curve you ask?
Here, it's just the average from a bunch of limited games.
